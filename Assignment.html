Here is quote from the happy puppy site.

Both of these dog breeds come from Japan. They each have triangular, pointed ears, and a fluffy curled tail that look quite fox-like!

Coat Colors

Akita's can have Ginger, Red, Brindle, or White. Shiba Inu's coats are Black and Tan, Cream, Sesame, Red Sesame, Black Sesame, and Red.

Both Dog Sizes

An akita stands 24 to 48 inches tall and a shiba inu stands 13 to 17 inches tall

The weight of an akita can be around 70 to 130 pounds while a shiba inu can weigh 15 to 25 pounds.
